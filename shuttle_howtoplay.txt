Hit shuttlecock with your racket, survive and reach the highscore!

Controls:
W A S D : `W` to jump/double jump. `S` to fastfall/crouch.
Space : Swing the racket
Esc : Pause

Tips:
- Immediately jumping after releasing crouch button will result in high jump.
- Hit the pink shuttlecock to score point
- Dodge the gray shuttlecock as it can't be reflected
- Don't hit the yellow shuttlecock with the racket, it can damage you
- In the menu, you can use either up/down arrow or W and S key to navigate
